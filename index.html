About Me

I was born and raised in St.Paul minnesota. And growing up cartoons and video games fueled my imagination. Which inspired me at a young age that it's what I wanted to do when I grew up. My down time consist of family, friends, kayaking, cartoons and video games. I've had a lot of great teachers over the years but none can compare to my sixth grade teacher, Mr Buetow, he was one of the strongest influences in my life that got me to where I am today. I've always wanted to have my own studio/company to either make cartoons or video games, it may take awhile to get there but thats my overall goal. Three words that best describe me Creative - My imagination is constantly coming up with stuff and I try to find new ways of doing tasks that eater make it more effestin or easier. Quick learner - I tend to understand things pretty quickly which helps me think of new ways to complete tasks. Dedicated - I hate leaving things unfinished and I like to see things through to the end.
